Pleated blinds
Pleated blinds

A modern alternative to traditional window blinds. They become more and more popular because of simplicity, versatility and attractive look.

They can be arranged freely starting from the top or bottom, each time achieving a different visual and functional effect. They can be easily installed, in a non-invasive manner using special hangers or in an invasive manner using small screws. Their main advantage is that they do not take too much space on the window wing allowing the windows to open easily. A wide range of colours of profiles and fabrics will satisfy the most demanding customer.
