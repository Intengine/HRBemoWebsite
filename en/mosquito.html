Mosquito nets

An irreplaceable solution during summer when we want to protect our rooms from various insects.

Thanks to this solution we can free open our windows. It is extremely convenient especially during summer heat when it allows us to additionally cool down the rooms. Mosquito nets can be adapted to suit any window dimensions, and select the colour of the frame according to our preferences. Mosquito nets can be mounted and dismantled fast and easily in a non-invasive manner for the window frame.
