ABOUT US
Our mission

Our mission is to implement timeless solutions.

Our main goal is meeting the expectations of our customers and the market as a reliable business partner.

Firma HR BEMO Sp. z o.o. was established in 2010. However, its origins date back to 1991 when Ireneusz Bednarz set up his first company specializing in the production of window blind systems.

Our experience

We have implemented a number of innovative solutions in our over 25-year history in an effort to meet changeable trends. Our strong position on the market enables us to adapt our products to meet the requirements of modern customers as regards technological, functional and aesthetic solutions.

We achieve the highest standards thanks to the continuous implementation of the state-of-the-art automated manufacturing process.

Our priorities

Innovative solutions, attention to the tiniest detail, competitive prices, convenient lead times, high product quality and continuous expansion of our product portfolio are our priorities.

Beside our permanent offer presented on this page, we are open to cooperation with customers seeking products dedicated to their needs. We offer a complex solution implementation service: from design to finished products.
